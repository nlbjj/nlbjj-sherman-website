Privacy Policy

Next Level BJJ Sherman LLC

Effective Date: September 25, 2025

Next Level BJJ Sherman LLC respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the data you provide.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information including:

  • Name, email, phone number, and address
  • Date of birth
  • Waiver and membership form data
  • Emergency contact information
  • Signature and participation data
  • Other details submitted via forms, applications, or communications

How We Use Your Information

We use the information to:

  • Process waivers, memberships, and event registrations
  • Communicate about classes, schedules, or member-related matters
  • Maintain accurate participant records
  • Ensure compliance with legal and safety obligations
  • Improve services and enhance the experience of members and visitors

How We Protect Your Information

We use secure storage systems and limit data access to authorized personnel. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except for legal or compliance reasons.

Cookies and Website Use

Our website may use cookies or similar tools to enhance your experience. These tools may collect general technical or usage data but are not used to capture sensitive personal information. You may disable cookies via your browser settings.

SMS Communications

Upon filling out applications and/or registering in our system, our clients consent to receive SMS from Next Level BJJ.

Important:

  • Reply STOP to opt-out
  • Reply HELP for support
  • Message and data rates apply
  • Messaging frequency may vary

SMS consent is not shared with third parties.

Data Access & Deletion Requests

You may request to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. For questions or data-related requests, contact us:
