Meet Our Instructors

World-Class Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu Instruction

Head Instructor & Founder

Professor Tony Eduardo Hoerhann

5th Degree Black Belt Master Rilion Gracie Lineage

Professor Tony Eduardo Hoerhann is a 5th-degree Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu black belt under Master Rilion Gracie, carrying one of the strongest lineages in the art. He is the founder of the Next Level Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu Association, guiding instructors and students worldwide through seminars, instructor development, and competition training.

Educational Background:

Tony holds a 4-year dual university degree in Physical Education from Brazil, combining Sports Science and Teaching. This program is equivalent to a U.S. Bachelor's degree‚Äîbut with full specialization from day one, without general education classes. His academic formation focused on human performance, biomechanics, motor learning, pedagogy, and youth development‚Äîwhich directly shapes the methodology and training philosophy at Next Level Jiu-Jitsu.

Competition Achievements:

  • South American Champion
  • South Brazilian Championships - Multiple titles
  • American Nationals - Championship titles
  • IBJJF World Championships (Mundials) - Podium finishes
  • International competition experience spanning decades

Teaching Experience:

  • Over two decades of teaching experience
  • International teaching in Brazil, United States, and worldwide
  • Long teaching stint in Shanghai, China
  • Founder of Next Level Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu Association
  • International seminar instructor and instructor developer

Mission & Philosophy:

"My mission is to bring the highest level of Jiu-Jitsu instruction to Sherman and to develop the next generation of martial artists through discipline, technique, and community. Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu is more than just a martial art - it's a way of life that builds character, discipline, and confidence."

At Next Level Sherman:

Professor Tony leads all classes and programs, sharing his extensive knowledge with students of every age and experience level. His commitment to excellence and authentic Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu instruction makes Next Level Sherman a premier training destination.

At Next Level Sherman:

Interested in Assisting?

We're always looking for dedicated practitioners who want to assist in classes and help others grow in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu. Please note: This is an assistant role - you will be supporting the main instructor, not leading classes independently.

Assistant Role Requirements:

  • Minimum Blue Belt rank
  • Passion for helping others and supporting instruction
  • Understanding that this is an assistant role, not independent teaching
  • Commitment to continuous learning under our instructors
  • Strong communication skills and ability to follow direction
  • Background check and references

Important Note:

Assistant roles involve supporting the main instructor during classes, helping students with techniques, and maintaining a safe training environment. You will not be leading classes independently but will be an important part of our teaching team.
