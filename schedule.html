Class Descriptions

🥋 Adult Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu

Comprehensive training covering fundamental techniques, positions, and live training. Perfect for beginners and experienced practitioners.

👶 Little Champions (Ages 4-7)

Fun introduction to BJJ focusing on basic movements, coordination, and character development in a safe environment.

🥋 Junior Warriors (Ages 8-12)

Structured BJJ training emphasizing technique, respect, and personal growth for school-age children.

⚡ Teen Elite (Ages 13-17)

Advanced training preparing teenagers for adult classes while maintaining age-appropriate instruction.

💪 No-Gi Training

Training without the traditional gi uniform, focusing on modern grappling and competition techniques.

🛡️ Self-Defense

Practical self-defense techniques based on BJJ principles for real-world situations.
