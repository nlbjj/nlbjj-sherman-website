Class Descriptions

🥋 Adult Fundamentals

Perfect for beginners! Learn the basic techniques, positions, and principles of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu in a supportive environment.

🥋 Adult ALL LEVELS

Comprehensive training for all skill levels. Advanced techniques, live training, and competition preparation for experienced practitioners.

🏆 Little Champs (Ages 5-9)

Fun introduction to BJJ focusing on discipline, movement patterns, and base control in a nurturing environment.

🥋 Junior Warriors (Ages 10-13)

Structured training focusing on drilling, technique precision, and situational sparring. Bridge to adult classes for mature students.

📅 Private Lessons

One-on-one instruction tailored to your specific goals, whether you're preparing for competition or focusing on particular techniques.
