About Next Level Jiu-Jitsu Sherman

Our Story

Next Level Jiu-Jitsu Sherman is part of the prestigious Next Level Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu Association (NLBJJA), bringing world-class Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu instruction to the heart of Sherman, Texas.

Founded on the principles of excellence, respect, and continuous improvement, our academy is dedicated to providing authentic Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu training for students of all ages and skill levels.

Located in the beautiful Midway Mall, our state-of-the-art facility offers a welcoming environment where students can develop not only their physical skills but also their mental discipline and character.
